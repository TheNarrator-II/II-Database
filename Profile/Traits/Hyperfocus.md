Always increases crafting quality and speed, as well as increasing the efficiency of menial tasks (chopping, mining) but you become Distracted as soon as you stop doing any of those actions.

During combat, you fight for your life, thus you're not Distracted during combat

(Distracted is a negative status effect that nullifies the vision and hearing feedback during perception checks/inspects, as well as leaving you more prone to surprise attacks. You do not become less prone if others warn you of incoming danger.)
